Shirts

Our shirts are made to evoke emotions from the wearer and viewer. We tend to use images that address the social injustices in the black community.

The more striking the Image the better for us. we want to promote social conciousness via thought provoking imagery. We believe that with visuals that shock people. It will start the process of thought for those who just see news as just news that doesnt affect them.

At the end of the day we just want everyone to take notice to the injustices that occur in this country.
